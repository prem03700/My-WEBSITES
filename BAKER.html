Our Story

Baked with Love in Tenali

Serving smiles since day one

Sasi Delicious Bakery is Tenali's beloved neighbourhood bakery and vegetarian restaurant, nestled in Ganganamma Peta. We believe every meal should be a celebration — from a simple morning tiffin to a beautifully crafted birthday cake.

We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally, to bring you baked goods and meals bursting with flavour. From flaky snacks to creamy cakes, hearty veg thalis to crispy masala dosas — there's something for every taste and occasion.

Our warm, inviting space is designed for families, friends, and food lovers. Whether you drop in for a quick snack, a full meal, or pick up a custom cake for your special day — we're here to make it delightful.

"Fresh ingredients. Familiar flavours. Family at heart." 🍰
